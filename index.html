Albert Einstein

He developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics

Here's a time line of Albert Einstein live:

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, in the Kingdom of Württemberg in the German Empire, on 14 March
  • 1897 - At 17, he enrolled in the four-year mathematics and physics teaching diploma program at the Zürich Polytechnic.
  • 1903 - Einstein and Marić get married
  • 1908 - He was recognized as a leading scientist and was appointed lecturer at the University of Bern.
  • 1911 - Einstein became a full professor at the German Charles-Ferdinand University in Prague in April
  • 1905 - Einstein postulated that light itself consists of localized particles (quanta) -- Photo-Electric Effect
  • 1921 - He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics "for his services to Theoretical Physics, and especially for his discovery of the law of the photoelectric effect"
  • 1933 - He emigrated to the U.S
  • 1940 - Einstein became an American citizen
  • 1955 - Einstein dies from internal bleeding caused by the rupture of an abdominal aortic aneurysm, at the age of 76

"He was almost wholly without sophistication and wholly without worldliness ... There was always with him a wonderful purity at once childlike and profoundly stubborn."

Nuclear Physicist Robert Oppenheimer
